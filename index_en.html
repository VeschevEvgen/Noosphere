FOR PRODUCERS OF UKRAINE

Conditions for R&D to enter into Joint Ventures in Europe
  • Availability of R&D that has the potential for rapid scaling.
  • Confirmation of previous R&D versions from the Ukrainian Military in real war.
  • Technical and technological documentation in English.
  • A representative office in Europe, which will be part of the Joint Venture.
  • Preferably - patented R&D in Europe (Ukraine has a ban on the export of drone technologies).
Joint Venture Responsibilities
  • Technology transfer.
  • Assistance in building a production or production system.
  • Organization of continuous testing in Ukraine under real war conditions.
  • Measures to modernize technologies and production.
Financial Benefits of Participating in a Joint Venture
  • Lump sum (base) contribution.
  • Partial or full payment for R&D.
  • Ownership of the assets of the Joint Venture (capitalization or dividends).
  • The right to R&D remains yours. Each new Joint Venture in a new country means additional profits.
